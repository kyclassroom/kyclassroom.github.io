Instructions

First, change the calculator setting:
  1. Press the "MODE" button 6 times. The "MODE" button is at the left of the "ON" button. You should see "Disp".
  2. Press "1". You should see "ab/c" and "d/c".
  3. Press "2".
After that, you can start the exercise. Here is an example:
QUESTION
Expand .
HOW TO DO
  1. The product of two degree-2 polynomials must be a degree-4 polynomial. (Think about why!) The coefficient of x4 is given by (1/2)(7/8) = 7/16. (Think about why!) Now you can write down 7/16 x4 as the first term.
  2. The coefficient of x3 is given by (1/2)(-9/10) + (-3/4)(7/8) = -177/160. (Think about why!) Now you can write down -177/160 x3 as the second term.
  3. The coefficient of x2 is given by (1/2)(11/12) + (-3/4)(-9/10) + (5/6)(7/8) = 149/80. (Think about why!) Now you can write down +149/80 x2 as the third term.
  4. Similarly, continue to write down -23/16 x +55/72 to finish the question.
When you finish the exercise, reload the page for another exercise until you are familiar with it.
